About Me

I am a Computer Science student at Nanjing Tech University with a passion for computer vision, robotics, and AI research. I am interested in intelligent computing and systems architecture, autonomous systems, human-centric AI, and cybersecurity. Educated in China, I merge multicultural perspective with robust technical expertise, enabling me to solve complex engineering and research challenges with creativity and adaptability.

My academic and project experiences span computer vision, robotics, embedded AI, and secure systems, where I have designed and implemented autonomous vehicle pipelines, intelligent perception modules, and academic-grade robotic projects. Proficient in Jetson Nano, ROS, YOLOv5, OpenCV, and embedded platforms, I specialize in turning theoretical models into deployable real-time solutions. I maintain strong interests in cybersecurity and intelligent system architectures, focusing on privacy-preserving AI, secure communication protocols, and resilience of autonomous systems against cyber threats.

I aim to contribute to advancing research in computer vision, intelligent robotics, and secure AI-driven systems, bridging the gap between cutting-edge innovation and real-world impact.
