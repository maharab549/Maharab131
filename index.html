About Me

I am a final-year Computer Science student at Nanjing Tech University (graduating June 2027), specializing in System Structure and Intelligent Computation and Artificial Intelligence. My research focuses on HW-SW co-design for efficient AI systems, edge computing architectures, and real-time intelligent systems.
